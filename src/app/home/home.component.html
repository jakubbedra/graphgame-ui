Welcome to graphgame!

Here you will find various tasks related to graph algorithms, that will help you learn for your upcoming exams.

Controls

Overall:

While holding the right mouse button on a vertex, you can move it freely across the board.

Drawing a graph:

Left click on empty space to add a vertex. Left click on a vertex to remove it. Left click and hold on a vertex in order to start drawing an edge. In order to finish drawing an edge, drag the mouse cursor to another vertex, and then release the left button.

Selecting vertices in order:

Left click on a vertex to select it. Left click on the last selected vertex to unselect it. The numbers inside the vertices represent the order in which computer generates those vertices

Selecting edges in order:

Left click on an edge to select it. Left click on the last selected vertex to unselect it. If you want to select more than one edge at once, right click the edge instead.

Vertex and edge coloring:

All vertices/edges have a default color assigned to them. In order to change the color click the right mouse button or the left mouse button.
